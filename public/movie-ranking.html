The Best Movies According to Mitch

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.

Phantom of the Opera

A disfigured musical genius who lives in the Paris Opera House becomes obsessed with a young soprano. However, love turns into a murderous rage when his young protege falls in love with another.

Leap Year

Anna Brady decides to travel to Dublin, Ireland, to propose to her boyfriend Jeremy on 29 February, a leap day. However, she faces several hindrances during the journey.
